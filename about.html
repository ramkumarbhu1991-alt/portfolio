About Dr. Ram Kumar

Mathematician · Educator · Researcher

Dr. Ram Kumar is a distinguished mathematician and educator currently serving as Assistant Professor-II in the Department of Mathematics at Amity University, Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh. His academic journey spans premier institutions across India, culminating in a doctoral degree from the Indian Institute of Technology (ISM), Dhanbad.

His research sits at the beautiful intersection of Number Theory, Algebraic Geometry, and the arithmetic of Elliptic Curves. With particular expertise in Hypergeometric Functions over Finite Fields, Dr. Kumar explores the profound connections between classical analysis and modern arithmetic geometry — a field of deep relevance to contemporary mathematics.

Beyond his research contributions, Dr. Kumar is deeply committed to fostering mathematical culture. He has organized multiple national and international workshops, secured prestigious research grants from NBHM and MPCST, and actively mentors students through competitive examination preparation and advanced mathematical training.
